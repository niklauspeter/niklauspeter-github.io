About Me

My educational background is not quite so different from that of the typical african boy-child. I was subjected to the same monotonous, ill defined 8-4-4 system. Performed slightly a bit more than a little bit above average in my KCPE exams, after which i joined high school. After completing my secondary school education in kagumo high school in Nyeri, i proceded to join Jommo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology where i completed a four year bachelors program and aquired a degree in business information technology.

During the period i got to work as an intern in gift computers, a computer repair organisation based in karen town, Nairobi. Where i can confidently claim to have aquired invaluable knowledge about the inner workings of the hardware elements of computers and computer related electronics. I also did part time free-lance writing during the time.

This exposure to this kind of environment motivated me to wanna know even more about how the computer is programmed, a lesson i felt i barely covered in campus. which is why, after a couple of recommendations from close counterparts on enquiry, i chose to join moringa school.
